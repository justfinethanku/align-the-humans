Our 5-Step Process

1

Setup

Choose a template and select your partner to begin the structured conversation process. We'll customize the form to match your specific needs.

2

Clarify

Our AI assistant will ask context questions about all topics listed and recommend additional areas of potential conflict based on your specific situation.

3

Answer

Each person independently answers the questions. Your responses are kept private until all parties have completed their answers.

4

Analyze

AI thoroughly compares the responses to find common ground, pinpoint areas of the most urgent areas where continued discussion may be useful.

5

Resolve

Iteratively compromise/negotiate genuinely such as the issues are given areas understood. AI suggests compromise options.
